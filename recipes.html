Vegetarian Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, sauté onions and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add diced bell pepper and cook until slightly softened.
  3. Stir in curry powder and cook for another minute.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, coconut milk, and chickpeas. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Serve with rice or naan bread.

Turkey and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • Assorted vegetables (such as broccoli, bell peppers, snow peas)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic powder
  • Ginger powder
  • Sesame oil
  • Cooked brown rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add ground turkey and cook until browned, breaking it into crumbles.
  3. Add chopped vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry until they're tender yet crisp.
  4. Season with soy sauce, garlic powder, and ginger powder to taste.
  5. Serve the stir-fry over cooked brown rice or quinoa.

Grilled Chicken Salad with Balsamic Dressing

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • Mixed greens (such as spinach, arugula, lettuce)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Balsamic vinaigrette dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange mixed greens on a serving platter.
  2. Top with grilled chicken slices, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and avocado.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Red onion, finely chopped
  • Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, chopped red onion, halved Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Add chopped fresh parsley and toss to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve chilled.

Vegetable and Tofu Stir-Fry with Brown Rice

Ingredients:

  • Firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, grated
  • Brown rice, cooked
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tofu cubes and cook until golden brown on all sides.
  4. Add assorted vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry until they're tender yet crisp.
  5. Season with soy sauce to taste and toss to combine.
  6. Serve the stir-fry over cooked brown rice, garnished with chopped green onions if desired.
